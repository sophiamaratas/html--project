Welcome to Coffee Co, where every cup of coffee is steeped in passion and history. Founded in 2023 by Sophia Maratas, our journey began with a simple dream: to create a gathering place for coffee lovers and a hub for community connection.

Our menu features an array of expertly crafted drinks, from classic espressos to our signature seasonal frappuccinos infused with local flavors. Don’t forget to try our freshly baked pastries, made daily with love!

In 2023, Sophia took a leap of faith and opened the doors to Coffee Co. in the heart of Talisay City. From day one, the mission has been clear: to serve high-quality, ethically sourced coffee while fostering a warm and welcoming environment.

At Coffee Co, we proudly source our beans from sustainable farms around the globe, ensuring each sip supports both quality and ethical practices.

We believe that coffee is more than just a drink; it’s a way to connect, share stories, and create memories. That’s why our shop is adorned with local art and hosts community events, making it a true gathering place for friends, families, and neighbors.

Join us at Coffee Co. and experience the love and passion that goes into every cup. We can’t wait to share this journey with you!
